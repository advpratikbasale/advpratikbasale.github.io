About Me

Registered Lawyer at Pune District Court

BA. LLB. (Pune University)

Advocate Pratik Basale is a renowned lawyer, enrolled in Good Standing of the State Bar Council of Maharashtra and Goa, Bar Council of Pune and Bar Council of Saswad. Currently practicing law in Pune district court, NCLT, DRT Court, Labour Court, SDO Court, Saswad Court, Motor Accidents Claims Tribunal (MACT) Revenue Courts across Maharashtra. He is currently pursuing a master's degree in law (LLM) from Pune University. He has obtained a BA. LLB. Degree from D.E.S. (Fergusson) Law College, Pune University.

With lot of passion towards Indian Law and Constitution, He regularly participates at legal aid events and takes up cases of people belonging to less privileged sections of society. He promises the work entrusted upon him will be executed and performed to the utmost capability of his skills and for the betterment of the Firm. The action will be result-oriented.
